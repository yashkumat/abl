About DSR Construction

Founded in 1985 by visionary Mr. Dharasingh Shivaji Rathod (B.E. Civil, COEP, 1982), DSR stands as a trusted name in the field of construction and contracting.

Mr. Dharasingh Rathod started his career with construction of the prestigious Kalidas Kala Mandir in Nashik, and did not look back since.

Over the period of 33 years, with his keen understanding of changing business dynamics, he nurtured a team of highly motivated, hard working and result oriented staff along with a large fleet of modern equipments and machinery.

It is no surprise that DSR has earned and retained the reputation of delivering projects with highest quality, best workmanship, competitive costing and time bound execution across India.

Having completed 100+ projects to our customer's satisfaction, we enjoy the pride of being a prime construction company.

DSR Today

Our current Director Mr. Prashant Dharasingh Rathod, following on the footsteps of Mr. Dharashing Rathod, is taking the company to newer heights, and expanding the company's footprint in roads, railways and industrial projects.

After completing his Civil Engineering from KK Wagh College of Engineering with Distinction in 2012, Darshan gained 5 years of ground experience on various project sites across India (Gujarat, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Rajasthan and Maharashtra). Form Work

We are first in Nashik to use Doka Shuttering which gives smooth ply finish to slabs. This finish reduces the cost for plastering as plaster is no longer needed.

We have extensive shuttering material of our own which gives us better advantage in competitive bidding and speedy execution.
