About ABL

Our Founding Vision

Founded in 1995 by Mr. Rajesh Kumar, ABL Construction began as a small civil works contractor with a vision to build infrastructure that transforms communities.

With a strong foundation in ethical business practices and engineering excellence, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary construction company executing projects across India.

Today, we employ over 500 professionals and skilled workers, operating with state-of-the-art equipment and technology to deliver projects on time and within budget.

Mr. Rajesh Kumar

Founder & Managing Director

ABL Today

Today, ABL Construction has diversified into various sectors including infrastructure, specialized works, and building structures, with an annual turnover exceeding ₹500 crores.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability in all our projects, from small-scale residential buildings to large infrastructure developments.
